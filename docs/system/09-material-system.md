# 09 — Material System (Active Concepts)

## 9.1 Philosophy: Ideas as Organisms
In WorldOS V3, a **Material** is not just a resource (like "Wood" or "Iron"). It is an **Active Concept** or "Meme" that lives, evolves, and exerts pressure on the world.
- *Examples*: "Rice Farming", "Confucianism", "Industrial Capitalism", "Gunpowder".

## 9.2 Structure of a Material

### 1. Ontology (Category)
- **Physical**: Tangible resources (e.g., *Iron*, *Coal*).
- **Institutional**: Social structures (e.g., *Feudalism*, *Banking*).
- **Symbolic**: Beliefs/Myths (e.g., *Divine Right*, *Nationalism*).
- **Behavioral**: Habits (e.g., *Ritualized Gift Giving*).

### 2. Lifecycle
Materials have a lifecycle in a Universe:
- **Dormant**: Exists as potential (in the "Tech Tree") but not active.
- **Active**: Discovered/Adopted. Exerts pressure on Physics.
- **Decayed/Obsolete**: Replaced by a superior Material (e.g., *Scrolls* -> *Books*).

## 9.3 The Pressure System
Materials are the "Muscles" of the simulation. They pull the Physics Vectors.

| Material | Input (Needs) | Output (Creates) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rice Farming** | Water, Low Entropy | Population Growth, Stability |
| **Industrialization** | Order, Innovation | Goods (Wealth), Pollution (Entropy), Inequality |
| **Secret Police** | High Authority | Order ↑, Terror (Trauma) ↑, Legitimacy ↓ |

## 9.4 Mutation Logic (Evolution)
Materials evolve via a **Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG)**.
- **Trigger**: "Innovation > 0.8" OR "Time > 500 years".
- **Path**: *Oral Tradition* -> *Writing* -> *Printing* -> *Digital Info*.
- **Divergence**: A Material can mutate differently based on World Physics (e.g., *Monarchy* in a High Magic world becomes *God-Emperor*; in a Low Magic world becomes *Constitutional Monarchy*).

## 9.5 Seeding (The Genetic Code)
The `AdvancedMaterialSeeder` injects the initial set of Materials based on the World's **Origin**.
- **Vietnamese Origin**: Seeds *Wet Rice Civilisation*, *Village Autonomy*, *Ancestor Worship*.
- **Western Origin**: Seeds *Wheat Farming*, *Feudal Hierarchy*, *Monotheism*.

This ensures every World starts with a unique "Cultural DNA" that evolves organically.
